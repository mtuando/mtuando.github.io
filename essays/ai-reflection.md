# Impact on Learning and Understanding

With proper balance on when to and not to use AI, I believe its contribution really strengthens my understanding of concepts, new and old. They managed to explain them easier than what I was taught in class or through online tutorials. Even though I try my best to not involve AI as much as possible, I don’t see any downsides to using without fully relying and manipulating them. Sometimes they don’t give me results that I desired to see, but the chance of that happening was low enough that I could care less about it.

# Practical Applications

Outside of the class, I had used AI in other ICS courses ranging from more theoretical courses like Data Structures and Algorithms and Security Science, to more practical courses like Computer Graphics and Data Visualizations. It breaks down concepts that went way over my head originally in class, into a language that makes it easier to understand for myself; it generates functionable code based on criterias I provided and can verify my and its codes. Like said from the previous section, I try to not fully be dependent on AI because it breaks the purpose of studying. Similar things can also be said for my General Education courses but there’s really no significance in me writing about them.

# Challenges and Opportunities

AI has only been relevant less than 5 years ago, and obviously there are still countless amounts of errors it can generate, though it should also be noted that the companies behind are working their best to narrow these margins of errors. 

On more than one occasion, I encountered the issue with limited use of my GPT, whether I reached the limit amount of messages, files, or even the thing stalls midway through. Various GPT do have their specific restrictions on how much a user can use their application, and if they want more then they better pay up for a Pro version to receive extra amounts of services. However, for a student like myself, paying for these has always been useless because there are always alternatives readily available for me to continue the conversation, and the extras they provide do not match well with their price.
